Starting Up

January 25th, 2016

We are so excited to have the first ever DEN meet-up tonight. Checkout the event page to learn more and follow along on Twitter with #DENLaunch to see links to future content.


Dyslexics are 10% of the population, 35% of entrepreneurs and 41% of prisoners. DEN is going to change the story of dyslexia, dyscalculia, ADHD and similar labels into one of strengths, not shame. With support, all members of DEN will be able to own who they are, understand it and, best of all, love it.


DEN is a community for individuals to learn and grow, and will be working towards making a difference in the world by advocating for research to further understand Dyslexia, working with partners to support Dyslexic entrepreneurs around the globe, and building relationship with government organizations to push for legislation that helps Dyslexic people.
